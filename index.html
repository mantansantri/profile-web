About Me

My name is Haikal Ammar Waluya, I was born in Surabaya, but now I live in Tangerang because of family matters. I am the first child of four siblings. I lived in Surabaya for only two years, then I moved to Tangerang and lived there. I started school in Tangerang when I was in the third grade of elementary school. The name of the SD is Pondok Ranji 03 SDN which is located in an area called Pertamina. After 4 years I went to school in Tangerang, I went back to school at an Islamic boarding school in Pandeglang called Riyadhussholihiin. After one year of studying there, I entered a department called Daar Asy-Syari'ah. There I fully studied religion and Arabic, not studying general subjects at all, unlike when I was in seventh grade, who also studied general subjects. So from that I became a person who is used to speaking Arabic. In fact, when I was there I used to speak Arabic and only sometimes I used Indonesian. Usually I speak Indonesian when I chat with my friends, the rest I use Arabic. But somehow my grades in Arabic are always bad. After I studied for 2 years at Daar Asy-Syari'ah, I decided to move to another school, because I wanted to find a new atmosphere. At first I wanted to go to Al-Furqon, Gresik. but because I heard from my cousin that it was uncomfortable there, I switched to IDN Jonggol. When I searched for IDN, I found that there is an IDN that is not as arid as in Jonggol. I finally decided to register at IDN solo and Alhamdulillah, I was accepted there.

UI/UX Designer

The UI design of a product aims to beautify the appearance of the product. While the UX design is designed to provide a pleasant experience when using the product. The focus of UI design is the beauty of the display, while UX focuses on product user satisfaction. A UI/UX designer is a professional who identifies new opportunities to create a better user experience. The role of UI/UX designers in the marketing and branding process of an application is very large, because they determine how users evaluate an application.

Android Developer

Android Developers can be said to be software developers who specialize in designing applications for the Android marketplace. In simple terms, the majority of their work is to create applications that can be used on smartphones or tablets, whether in the form of games or other forms of applications. Mobile app developers play a role in building products in the form of mobile-based applications, generally consisting of a group of programmers who have been trained and are familiar with it. Their work focuses on 'translating' ideas from the field of design and application of software engineering.
